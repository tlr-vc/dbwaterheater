Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a water heater installation take?

Most tank water heater installations are completed in 2-4 hours. Tankless installations may take 4-6 hours depending on the complexity. We complete most installations the same day you call.

Do I need a permit for water heater installation?

Yes, permits are typically required for water heater installations in California. We handle all permit requirements and ensure the installation meets local building codes.

What's the difference between tank and tankless water heaters?

Tank water heaters store hot water in a reservoir and are more affordable upfront. Tankless heaters heat water on-demand, are more energy-efficient, and never run out of hot water. We'll help you choose the right option for your home and budget.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! We specialize in same-day water heater installation and replacement. If you call us in the morning, we can often have your new water heater installed by the end of the day.

What warranty comes with my new water heater?

Water heaters come with manufacturer warranties (typically 6-12 years depending on the model). We also guarantee our installation workmanship and will address any installation-related issues.

Do you haul away my old water heater?

Yes, we include removal and disposal of your old water heater in our service. We'll haul it away so you don't have to worry about it.

How do I know what size water heater I need?

We'll help you choose the right size based on your household size, hot water usage, and plumbing setup. Most homes need a 40-50 gallon tank or an appropriately sized tankless unit.
