Tankless Water Heater Questions

How much does tankless installation cost compared to a tank?

Tankless water heaters have higher upfront costs but lower operating costs. The energy savings typically pay for the difference within 5-7 years. We'll provide a detailed quote after assessing your home's specific requirements.

Gas or electric tankless - which is better?

Gas tankless heaters provide higher flow rates and are ideal for larger households. Electric tankless units work well for smaller homes or point-of-use applications. We'll recommend the best option based on your needs and existing infrastructure.

Can I retrofit a tankless system in my existing home?

Yes, most homes can be retrofitted with tankless systems. We'll assess your gas lines, electrical capacity, and venting requirements to ensure proper installation. Some upgrades may be needed depending on your home's current setup.

How much energy will I actually save?

Most homeowners save 20-30% on water heating costs with tankless systems. For a typical household spending $400-600/year on water heating, that's $80-180 in annual savings. Over the unit's 20+ year lifespan, the savings are substantial.

Do tankless water heaters require maintenance?

Tankless units require annual descaling/flushing, especially in areas with hard water. This simple maintenance helps maintain efficiency and extends the unit's lifespan. We can provide maintenance service or show you how to do it yourself.
