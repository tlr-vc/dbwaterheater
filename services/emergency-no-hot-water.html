Emergency Service Questions

How quickly can you respond?

We prioritize emergency calls and can typically arrive same-day if you call in the morning. Our goal is to get your hot water running as quickly as possible. Call us now to schedule your emergency service.

Do you offer after-hours emergency service?

We focus on same-day service during business hours. If you call us early in the day, we can usually get you taken care of before evening. For truly urgent situations, call us and we'll do our best to help.

How do you diagnose the problem?

We'll inspect your water heater, check the pilot light (gas), heating elements (electric), thermostat, and look for signs of leaks or corrosion. In most cases, we can quickly identify whether repair or replacement is the best option.

Repair or replace - how do you decide?

If your water heater is under 8 years old and the repair cost is reasonable, we'll usually recommend repair. If it's older or needs extensive work, replacement is often more cost-effective. We'll explain your options and help you decide.

Can you replace the water heater the same day?

Yes! If replacement is needed, we typically have common sizes in stock and can complete installation the same day. You'll have hot water back by the end of the day.
