Introduction

DB Water Heater ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Doughty Brothers Construction, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Contact Information: When you call, text, or email us for a quote or service, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and service address.

Service Information: Details about your water heater service needs, appointment scheduling, and service history.

Website Usage: Basic information about how you use our website, such as pages visited and time spent on the site.

Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This includes information like pages visited, time spent on site, and traffic sources. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data anonymously.

We use your information to:

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights
